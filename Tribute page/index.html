Here's a timeline of Elon Musk's life.

  • 1971 - Born in South Africa. At age Creates and writes a video game called Blastar; sells it for the equivalent of $500
  • 1988 - Graduates from Pretoria Boys High School with distinctions in science and computer studies.
  • 1989 to 1991 - Attends college at Queenâ€™s University in Kingston, Ontario. Then transfers to the University of Pennsylvania; completed a BS in Economics (Wharton) and a BA with a major in physics.
  • 1995 - Moves to Silicon Valley; defers graduate program in applied physics and materials science at Stanford University to join the Internet boom
  • 1999 - Sells Zip2 to Compaq, the personal computer company, for $307 million, of which $22 million went to Musk. Then forms X.com, which in 2000 morphs into PayPal
  • 2002 - eBay acquires PayPal for $1.5 billion in stock, of which $165 million goes to Musk. Becomes an American citizen, founds SpaceX.
  • 2004 - Invests in Tesla Motors and later in 2008 becomes Tesla's CEO.
  • 2012 - SpaceX becomes the first commercial vehicle to deliver a load of supplies to the International Space Station. Tesla begins deliveries of the all-electric Model S.
  • 2013 - SolarCity became the second largest solar power provider. Releases sketch and concept of the Hyperloop.
  • 2015 - Announced creation of OpenAI, an Artificial Intelligence company. SpaceX began development of the Starlink constellation of low-Earth-orbit satellites to provide satellite Internet access.
  • 2016 - Revealed Mars colonization exploration architecture details.
  • 2017 - Hyperloop One announced first successful test run. Purchased x.com domain from PayPal.
  • 2018 - Left the OpenAI board. Attempted to provide assistance to rescuers during the Tham Luang cave rescue. Warned about the dangers of AI.
  • 2019 - Traveled to China for the groundbreaking of Tesla's Shanghai Gigafactor
  • 2020 - Elon Musk has topped Microsoft founder Bill Gates to become the world's second richest person. Tesla launched Y crossover.
  • 2021 - The Boring Company completed a tunnel beneath the las vegas convention center.
  • 2022 - Musk sold $3.6 billion of his stock in Tesla, equal to 22 million shares in the company. He acquired Twitter for $44 billion, merged the company into newly created X Corp. and rebranded the service as X the following year.
  • 2023 - Elon Musk launched an artificial intelligence company called xAI, which aims to develop a generative AI program that competes with existing offerings like ChatGPT.
  • 2024 - Ascended to the top of the global wealth ladder, becoming the richest person in the world.
